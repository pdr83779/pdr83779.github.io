Happiness

Happiness, in my opinion, is an emotion of constant joy and comfort. It brings peace within and allows for you to live life with a smile. You can find happiness in certain aspects of life but to be genuinely happy, you must find happiness within yourself. Pure happiness is to be content with your life. You are able to conquer any obstacle life throws your way with strength and grace. You are capable of overcoming anything because you know your strengths and weaknesses and how to use them to your advantage. To be purely happy is to know who you are and take measures to accommodate to your needs as a healthy and joyful human being.

My Low

Throughout the years, I struggled to find happiness from within. Pure happiness became such a mythical topic to me. Getting out of bed in the morning was my biggest struggle. Some days, the thought of smiling just seemed so bizarre and far-fetched. I lived life bad day after bad day until I just couldn’t handle it anymore. After putting on a fake smile to my peers for so many years, I finally accepted my flaws and decided to change my life drastically in order to accommodate my mental needs.

My High

Beginning my new regime of happiness, I first began to eat healthy. Eating healthy is more than just beneficial for your physical health but it is also beneficial for your mental health. A healthy body produces a healthy mindset. Next, I began to exercise frequently. I did yoga every week in hopes of it bringing happiness to my life. The weekly yoga sessions motivated me to participate in daily meditation practices. Every day when I wake up, I meditate for 10 minutes in order to start my day with a happy and fresh outlook. Another thing I do is SMILE! Smiling even when you are in a bad mood helps uplift your attitude. It brings joy to the situation and thus bringing more happiness to the table! I also realized that always being positive increases overall happiness. If you are having a bad day, think about something good that happened! Dropped your ice cream? It’s okay! Ice cream has tons of sugar and is very unhealthy for your body anyways! Failed a test? It is okay! You are a happy and healthy human being and you should be grateful that you are even able to pursue a collegiate education! On this journey of happiness, I realized that I can only do so much. I can change my daily habit in order to produce more happiness but I also must surround myself with those who uplift me. A big key to being happy is to have friends who are happy and positive.
